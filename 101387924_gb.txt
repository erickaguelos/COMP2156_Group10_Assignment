1.	George Brown College was founded in 1968.

2.	George Brown College is a college of applied arts and technology providing education in many public areas with three main campuses located in downtown Toronto.
3.	The college is named after a Scottish-born Canadian politician and newspaper publisher in the 19th century.

4.	The college has three campuses, Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront, and is specifically designed to host various academic programs and extracurricular activities.
