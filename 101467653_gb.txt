                        ###
                George Brown College 

George Brown (1818-1880) was a 19th-century Scottish-born Canadian politician, newspaper publisher (he founded The Globe, precursor to The Globe and Mail) 
and was one of the Fathers of Confederation. An essential figure in the formation of Canada, Brown was a strong believer in public education.

George Brown offers more than 170 full-time program in art and design, business, community services, 
early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, health sciences, hospitality
and culinary arts, preparatory studies as well as specialized programs and services.
